Our Story

Nisi Restaurant

Nisi Restaurant was founded in 1999 by Xhenis, who had a vision of creating a dining experience that celebrates the rich culinary traditions of Albania and around the world while incorporating modern influences. Our journey began with a small, passionate team and a handful of cherished family recipes. Over the years, we have grown and evolved, but our dedication to quality and authenticity has never wavered. Our menu is a reflection of our love for food and our commitment to excellence. We source our ingredients from local farms and trusted suppliers, ensuring that every dish we serve is fresh, flavorful, and of the highest quality. From our signature MEAT LOAF to our seasonal specials, each item on our menu is crafted with care and attention to detail. Beyond the food, Nisi Restaurant is about community. We believe that a great meal is about more than just what’s on the plate – it’s about the people you share it with. That’s why we’ve created a welcoming space where friends and family can gather to enjoy good food, good company, and good times. Thank you for being a part of our story. We look forward to serving you and making you a part of our extended family.
